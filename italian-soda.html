Italian Soda

Home

Ingredients

  • 4 tlbs flavored syrup
  • 1 cup ice
  • 16 oz club soda
  • 1 tlbs cream (heavy or whipped) optional
  • Other toppings like limes, lemons, fruit

Directions

  1. Using a tall clear glass, add in any combination of flavored syrups about 3 tablespoon of syrup total.
  2. Add in ice.
  3. Fill with club soda or sparking water.
  4. Top with a little drizzle of heavy cream or mound of whipped cream.
  5. Top with a toppings coordinating with your flavors.
  6. Swirl gently with a straw before drinking.
